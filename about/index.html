About

Our mission

In an increasingly complex and uncertain world, making the right decision for your business or identifying the best next product to launch has never been so challenging. At Prophet AI, we build novel AI algorithms that empower businesses to optimize their critical processes and quantify the associated risk. Our models are specifically designed to target extreme outcomes—both positive, for maximum added value, and negative, to help you be better prepared.

Our story

Prophet AI stems out of nearly a decade of extreme and rare event uncertainty quantification. Our research showed that carefully crafted physics-based models are sensitive to assumptions and noise, thus impossible to implement in practice. Model agnostic digital twins appeared as a flexible solution and we have efficiently characterized 15+ unique problems (from path planning, to turbulent flow control, to ship design), demonstrated through several academic journals.
